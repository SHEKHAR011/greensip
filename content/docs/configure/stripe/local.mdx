---
title: How to Whisk Matcha (Usucha)
description: Simple method for smooth foam and texture
---

## Step-by-Step

<div className="steps">
  <div className="step">
    Sift 2 grams matcha into a warm bowl.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Add 60 to 70 ml hot water at 75C to 80C.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Whisk in a fast M or W motion for 15 to 20 seconds.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Slow down and finish with small circles to polish the foam.
  </div>
</div>

Drink immediately for the freshest aroma.
