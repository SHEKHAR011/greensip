---
title: How to Make Matcha Latte
description: Creamy latte method for daily drinks
---

## Ingredients

- 2 grams matcha
- 50 ml hot water (75C to 80C)
- 150 ml milk or oat milk
- Optional sweetener

## Method

<div className="steps">
  <div className="step">
    Whisk matcha with hot water until smooth.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Steam or heat milk separately.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Pour milk into matcha and stir gently.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    Add sweetener if needed and serve.
  </div>
</div>
