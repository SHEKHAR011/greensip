---
title: Matcha Brewing Workflow
description: End-to-end routine from prep to serving
---

Use this workflow when you want reliable matcha every time.

## Prep

1. Warm your bowl and whisk with hot water.
2. Dry the bowl lightly.
3. Sift 2 grams matcha into the bowl.

## Brew

1. Add 60 to 70 ml water at 75C to 80C.
2. Whisk in an M or W motion for 15 to 20 seconds.
3. Finish with short circular strokes to smooth the foam.

## Serve Options

- Drink straight as usucha
- Pour over ice for a cold version
- Add steamed milk for a latte

## Consistency Tips

- Keep your ratio constant for repeatable taste
- Taste and adjust only one variable at a time
- Clean tools right after use to avoid stale aroma
