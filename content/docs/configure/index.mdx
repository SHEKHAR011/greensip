A simple setup is enough to make excellent matcha.

## Recommended Starter Kit

- Matcha powder (ceremonial or premium)
- Bamboo whisk (chasen)
- Matcha bowl (chawan)
- Bamboo scoop or teaspoon
- Fine mesh sifter
- Kettle with temperature control
