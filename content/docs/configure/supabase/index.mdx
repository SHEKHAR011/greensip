---
title: Matcha Powder Guide
description: How to choose quality matcha
---

Use brighter green powder for smoother taste and less bitterness.

## Grade Quick Guide

- **Ceremonial grade**: Best for drinking straight with water
- **Premium grade**: Great for daily bowls and lattes
- **Culinary grade**: Best for baking and desserts

## Buying Tips

- Check harvest origin and date
- Avoid dull yellow-green powder
- Prefer airtight tins over clear packaging
