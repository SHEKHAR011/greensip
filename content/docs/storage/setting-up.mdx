title: Brewing Technique
description: Build consistency in every cup
---

## Core Ratio

Start with 2 grams matcha and 60 to 70 ml water.

## Technique Notes

1. Always sift to remove clumps.
2. Keep water below boiling.
3. Whisk from the wrist, not the shoulder.
4. Stop as soon as microfoam appears.

## Common Mistakes

- Using boiling water
- Skipping the sifter
- Whisking too slowly
- Letting matcha sit too long before drinking
