---
title: Iced Matcha Example
description: Refreshing iced method for warm days
---

## Ingredients

- 2 grams matcha
- 50 ml warm water (75C to 80C)
- Ice
- 120 ml cold water or milk

## Steps

1. Sift and whisk matcha with warm water.
2. Fill a glass with ice.
3. Pour whisked matcha over ice.
4. Top with cold water or milk.
5. Stir and enjoy.
