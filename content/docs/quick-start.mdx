## What You Need

- 2 grams ceremonial or premium matcha
- 60 to 70 ml hot water (about 75C to 80C)
- Chawan (bowl)
- Chasen (bamboo whisk)
- Fine sifter

<div className="steps">
  <div className="step">
    **Sift matcha**: Add 2 grams of matcha through a fine sifter into your bowl.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    **Add warm water**: Pour 60 to 70 ml water at 75C to 80C.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    **Whisk quickly**: Use an M or W motion for 15 to 20 seconds.
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    **Serve immediately**: Drink as-is, or top with milk for a latte.
  </div>
</div>
