---
title: Matcha Troubleshooting
description: Fix taste and texture issues quickly
---

## Bitter Taste

- Lower water temperature
- Use fresher powder
- Reduce whisk time slightly

## Clumps

- Sift before adding water
- Start with a small splash of water to make a paste

## Weak Flavor

- Increase powder to 2.5 grams
- Use less water

## Too Thick

- Add 10 to 20 ml warm water and whisk again
