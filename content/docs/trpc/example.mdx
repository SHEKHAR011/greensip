description: Easy ways to customize your matcha

## Popular Add-ons

- Vanilla syrup
- Yuzu juice
- Honey
- Mint
- Coconut water

## Pairing Ideas

- Matcha + oat milk + vanilla
- Matcha + yuzu + sparkling water
- Matcha + honey + cinnamon

Keep additions light so the tea profile stays present.
