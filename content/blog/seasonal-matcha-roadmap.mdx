Here is our seasonal plan for GreenSip so you know what to expect and when.

## Spring: Clean and Bright

**Focus:** fresh, crisp profiles for warmer mornings.

- Limited citrus-forward can drop
- "starter matcha" tasting notes for new drinkers
- short guide: iced serving tips without dilution

## Summer: Cold Rituals

**Focus:** hydration-friendly pairings and afternoon reset energy.

- low-sugar flavor trials
- cafe-style serving suggestions over ice
- community favorites voting for permanent rotation

## Autumn: Rich and Comforting

**Focus:** deeper umami and cozy pairings.

- roasted note blend experiments
- breakfast pairing guide (oats, pastries, fruit)
- behind-the-scenes sourcing updates

## Winter: Intentional Energy

**Focus:** steady focus and simple routines.

- ritual bundles for work-from-home and study
- practical "coffee-to-matcha" transition guide
- year-end flavor recap and next-year preview

## How We Choose What Ships

Every release is evaluated on:

1. flavor balance,
2. clean ingredient fit,
3. repeat drinkability,
4. and how well it supports everyday energy.

If a flavor is exciting but not sustainable for daily use, we keep iterating.
