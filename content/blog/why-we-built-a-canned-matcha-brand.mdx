We built GreenSip around one simple problem: most energy drinks spike hard, then crash hard.

We wanted something cleaner, steadier, and enjoyable enough to drink every day.

## The Gap We Saw

People already loved matcha, but daily routines were blocked by friction:

1. whisking tools,
2. prep time,
3. inconsistent quality,
4. and inconvenient portability.

## Our Answer: Premium Matcha in a Can

We focused on four principles:

- **Ceremonial-grade matcha first** for real taste and quality.
- **Balanced flavor** so it is smooth and repeatable.
- **Clean ingredients** with no unnecessary complexity.
- **Ready-to-drink convenience** for commuting, work, and workouts.

## Why It Matches Everyday Energy

Matcha naturally contains caffeine and L-theanine, a combination many people experience as focused and smoother than coffee spikes.

Our mission is not "maximum stimulation." It is reliable, feel-good energy you can build habits around.
