A full matcha ritual does not need to be complicated. This setup takes about 10 minutes and works for most homes.

## Essentials You Need

1. Matcha bowl (chawan)
2. Bamboo whisk (chasen)
3. Fine sieve
4. Warm water (not boiling)
5. Your favorite GreenSip canned matcha for quick days

## Morning 10-Minute Ritual

### Minute 1-2: Warm and Prepare

- Warm your bowl and whisk with hot water.
- Empty and dry lightly.

### Minute 3-4: Sift Matcha

- Sift 1-2 teaspoons into the bowl.
- This prevents clumps and improves foam.

### Minute 5-6: Add Water

- Use water around 70-80 C.
- Add a small splash first and make a smooth paste.

### Minute 7-8: Whisk

- Whisk in a quick "M" motion.
- Aim for tiny bubbles and a glossy top.

### Minute 9-10: Choose Your Format

- Drink ceremonial-style at home, or
- switch to a chilled GreenSip can when leaving for work.

## When to Use a Canned Option

Use canned matcha when:

- you are commuting,
- have back-to-back meetings,
- or want consistent quality without prep.

This is how a ritual becomes daily, not occasional.

## Keep It Sustainable

- prep tools the night before,
- keep your favorite can flavor stocked,
- and pair matcha with a light snack for better balance.

Consistency beats complexity every time.

## Brand Tip From GreenSip

If you are just starting, keep one simple rule: ceremonial at home, canned on the move. That balance keeps your routine realistic and enjoyable.
